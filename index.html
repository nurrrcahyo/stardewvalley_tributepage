The Man Behind Stardew Valley

About Eric Barone

The man, a single man, behind one of the best game ever created, the Stardew Valley. Out of his struggle to land a job, he creates a video game. By creates, I mean doing the code, illustrating the visual in pixel style, writing the plot, developing the characters, composing the music, all by himself for over 4 years. The website says that the game has been aready sold over 41 million copies across all platform.

Works

There is not much that the internet knows about his works but two. One of them is that gem, Stardew Valley, and the other is the upcoming Haunted Chocolatier.

Stardew Valley

Released on 2016, the game is about creating nostalgia of him playing the game most of us milleniasls love, the Harvest Moon. But it's not just about sowing seeds and feeding the cattles. It's more than that. If Harvest Moon is about farming life and social interaction, it adds two more aspect; combat and crafting.

Haunted Chocolatier

So little known about this particular title. However, as the teaser video Ape posted on 2021, the artstyle is the same as its sibling title. And what I can understand is that the premise revolves around a man who runs a chocolate factory that the indregients he needs must be collected by fighting monsters. Well, let him cook.

Why He Inspired Me

One man army who struggled to find a job and chose to do what he loves instead and ends up getting wealthy. How can it not inspire a man like me? The dedication to work 10 hours a day, 7 days a week for over 4 years, it's magical. I hope I can at least imitate his work ethics.
